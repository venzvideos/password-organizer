About this Vault

This is a private, local-only account organizer. Every account you add — Facebook, Gmail, Discord, Steam, whatever you use — gets sorted by category, with its own logo, and stays hidden behind a PIN until you choose to reveal it.

Nothing here is sent anywhere. Everything you save lives only in this browser's local storage on this device.
